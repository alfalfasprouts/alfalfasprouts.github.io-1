Sangeeta N. Bhatia

Country — United States
Field — Science and Technology

Sangeeta Bhatia is trained as both a physician and an engineer, and leads a laboratory dedicated to leveraging miniaturization tools from the world of semiconductor manufacturing to impact human health at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Her multidisciplinary team of doctors and technologists has built countless inventions targeting everything from liver disease to cancer. Her work has been profiled in Scientific American, BBC News Forbes, The Economist, TEDMED, and more.

Alma Harel

Country — Israel
Field — Film and Media Arts

Alma Harel is an award-winning Israeli-American music video and film director. She is famous for her ability to artistically blur the lines between documentary and fiction, effectively utilizing choreographed dance sequences and inspired musical choices in a surreal, dream-like poetic meditation on life. In addition to creating commercial work for brands like Airbnb, Facebook and P&G, she has taught at Harvard’s Sensory Ethnography Lab and NYU.

Shiina Ringo

Country — Japan
Field — Music and Performance

Shiina Ringo is a Japanese singer, songwriter and musician. She has had a boundary-pushing career spanning two decades, and has written, composed, and arranged music for many other Japanese artists as well. She recently directed the flag handover at the closing ceremony of the Rio 2016 Olympic Games, and is part of the team in charge of the Opening and Closing Ceremonies for the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

Selly Raby Kane

Country — Senegal
Field — Fashion and Design

Selly Raby Kane is a Senegalese fashion designer and a central figure in a new wave of creative practitioners in Dakar, Senegal's capital. She has developed an eclectic, uninhibited style that owes as much to street artist Banksy as it does to her love of traditional West African textiles. Her work has been featured in fashion magazines worldwide, and is known for her boundary-pushing designs which mesh artistic elements from cultures all over the world.
